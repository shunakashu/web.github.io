Here is the collection of links which I find useful (lecture notes, YouTube videos, event HPs, etc.)

CFT lecture notes

  • By Ginsparg in 1988 (Les Houches): a renowned lecture note, still very useful today
  • By Estinne & Ikhref in 2023: relatively short and easy-to-read review
  • By Northe in 2024: nice introduction for boundary & defect CFT
  • By Affleck in 2008: BCFT approach to impurity problem (condmat-friendly)

Generalized Symmetry lecture notes

  • By Sakura in 2023 (ICTP): details about gauging, and contains higher-dimensional extension

    • YouTube videos lecture 1 lecture 2 lecture 3 lecture 4

  • By Shao in 2023 (TASI): very helpful with many examples (especially of Ising CFT), but also contains half-gauging

    • YouTube videos lecture 1 lecture 2 lecture 3 lecture 4

  • By Justin in 2026 (Kyoto): in my opinion this is the best introduction ever for symmetry TFT construction
  • By Kong & Zhang in 2022: focusing on categorical aspect of symmetry (with motivation for topological order), with many pictures and diagrams

  • Juven Wang's YouTube channel
